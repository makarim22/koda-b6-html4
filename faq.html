Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries answered about our carsharing service

General Questions

1. What is carsharing?

Carsharing is a service that allows members to use a vehicle for a short period of time, typically by the hour or day. It offers flexibility and convenience for those who do not need a vehicle full-time.

2. How does it work?

Simply sign up on our website, book a vehicle through our app, and unlock the car using your smartphone. You will only pay for the time you use the vehicle, including fuel and insurance.

3. Who can join?

Anyone aged 21 or older with a valid driving license can join our carsharing service. Additional requirements may apply based on your location.

4. How are fees calculated?

Fees are calculated based on the duration of your rental and the type of vehicle. Additional fees may apply for fuel, insurance, and mileage. You can view the pricing details when booking a vehicle through our app.

5. What should I do in case of an accident?

If an accident occurs, please remain calm and ensure everyone's safety first. Report the incident to the authorities if necessary and contact our support team immediately for further instructions.

Booking & Cancellation

6. How do I book a car?

You can book a car through our mobile app or website. Simply select your desired location, choose a vehicle, and select your rental period.

7. Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking through the app or website up to 24 hours before your rental period starts without any fees. Cancellations made closer to the reservation time may incur a fee.

8. Is there a limit to how many cars I can book at once?

Yes, you can book one vehicle at a time while your booking is active. Once you return the vehicle, you can book another car.
